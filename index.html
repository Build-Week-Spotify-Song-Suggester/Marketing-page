Spotifier

Connecting users with songs they didn't know they loved!

Spotifier allows the user to play songs based on similar songs that the listener has listened to, or their mood.

Features

  • Save your favorite songs to your profile.
  • Makes suggestions based on audio features on your profile.
  • You can choose suggestions based on your mood.
  • Add and edit songs on your playlist.
